The order goes...

Highest(1): !, ++, -- Evaluated from right to left
(2): *, /, % Evaluated from left to right
(3): +, - Evaluated from left to right
(4): <,>,<=,>= Evaluated from left to right
(5): ==, != Evaluated from left to right
(6): &&
(7): !!
Lowest(8): =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %= Evaluated from right to left
